Frequently Asked Questions

Why is a wired ethernet connection better than Wi-Fi?

A hardwired Cat6 connection delivers consistent gigabit speeds with zero wireless interference, no signal drops, and dramatically lower latency. For gaming, video calls, or any device that demands reliable connectivity, there is no substitute.

Do you fish the cable through the wall or run it along the baseboard?

I fish cables through walls, attics, and crawl spaces for a completely hidden run. I do not staple cables along baseboards. The finished result looks like the port was built into the wall originally.

What is included per drop?

Each drop includes the cable run, wall plate installation on both ends, and a speed test to confirm full gigabit throughput before I leave.
