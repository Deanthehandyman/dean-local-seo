Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from Wi-Fi Mesh or Starlink?

Wi-Fi Mesh extends your home internet network. Starlink provides internet via satellite. A cellular booster strengthens your phone's direct cellular signal — useful even if you have great Wi-Fi but bad cell reception, like in a shop or barn away from the house.

Will this work on my property if I barely get any signal at all?

In most cases yes, as long as there's some usable signal outside to amplify. I'll do a signal check before booking if you're unsure — text me at 281-917-9914.

Does this work for all carriers?

Most boosters are carrier-agnostic and work across major carriers simultaneously, which I can confirm for your specific situation.
