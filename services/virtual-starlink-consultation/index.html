Frequently Asked Questions

What can you actually fix or diagnose remotely?

In most cases I can identify the root cause of slow speeds, dropped connections, obstruction issues, router placement problems, and app configuration errors entirely over video. I will tell you honestly if an on-site visit is needed.

What do I need for the video call?

A smartphone, tablet, or computer with a working camera and internet connection. I will send you a video link after booking. Being near your Starlink router and dish during the call is helpful.

Is the consultation fee credited toward an on-site install?

Yes. If the consultation leads to a booked on-site installation, the consultation fee is credited toward your install cost.
