Frequently Asked Questions

Will the cables actually be hidden, or just bundled?

I run cables through the wall cavity between the TV and a recessed power outlet whenever the wall allows it. This is a true concealed run, not just a cable sleeve.

Can you mount on brick, stone, or over a fireplace?

Yes. These mounts need additional anchoring and care, and I'll assess on site since material and wall type affect what hardware is needed.

Do you set up the TV itself (streaming devices, apps)?

I can pair streaming devices and do basic setup at your request. For deeper smart home integration, that's a separate Smart Home Device Setup visit.
