Typical duration120 min

A thorough walk-through of your RV's critical systems before a big trip or before you buy a used rig. I check electrical connections, battery condition, propane line connections, slide-out mechanisms, roof seals, and shore power hookup — and give you a clear, honest written rundown of what's solid and what needs attention. As a full-time RVer myself, I know what actually breaks down on the road.

Heads up: the date and time you select is your requested appointment slot. I'll call or text you within a few hours of booking to confirm details and discuss what you need checked.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you inspect an RV I'm about to buy from someone else?

Yes. Pre-purchase inspections are common and can save you from a costly surprise. I'll meet you at the seller's location if needed.

Do you check the engine or drivetrain on motorhomes?

No — this inspection covers RV-specific systems (electrical, propane, slides, seals, hookups). Engine and drivetrain inspection should go through a mechanic.

What if you find something that needs repair?

I'll tell you straight what I found. If it's something I can fix, we can schedule that separately. If it needs a specialist, I'll point you in the right direction.
