Frequently Asked Questions

Why would I need a ground pole instead of a roof mount?

Trees, barns, rooflines, and nearby structures can block the satellite signal Starlink needs. A ground pole lets me position the dish at the exact height and angle needed for a clear, unobstructed sky view on your specific property.

What is included in the ground pole installation?

I supply and set a steel ground pole in concrete, mount the Starlink dish at the optimal position, and route the cable cleanly back to your home or RV. The pole is permanent and built to handle East Texas weather.

Do you also connect the internet inside my home?

Yes. The ground pole mount is typically booked alongside a full Starlink installation. I run the cable inside and complete the full Wi-Fi network setup so everything is working before I leave.

Choose the option that matches what you have on hand: your own kit and pole/mounting hardware, just the Starlink kit with me supplying the pole and hardware, or let me source everything including the dish itself.
