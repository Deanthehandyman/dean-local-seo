Frequently Asked Questions

Is this the same as your Starlink Installation service?

No — this covers shore power and surge protection only. If you also want Starlink installed on your RV, book that separately or mention it when you contact me so we can schedule both in one visit.

Do I need a 30-amp or 50-amp surge protector?

This depends on your RV's electrical system. I'll confirm your amperage and recommend the right protector during the visit.

Can you also help with a permanent RV pad electrical setup?

Yes — if you're developing a homestead or RV pad and need a dedicated power run, mention this when booking so I can scope the full job.
