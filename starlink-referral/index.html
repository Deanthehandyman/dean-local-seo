Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Starlink referral work?

When you use my referral link to order Starlink, both you and I get one free month of service credit. The credit is applied automatically after your first full billing cycle. It works for new Residential subscribers only.

Can I use the referral link if I already have Starlink?

No — the referral only applies to brand-new Starlink accounts. If you already subscribe, the link won't apply a credit, but I can still handle your hardware installation, cable routing, or ground pole mount.

Does the referral work with all Starlink plans?

The referral applies to the standard Residential plan. Business, Maritime, and RV plans are not included in the referral program.

Do I have to book an install through Dean's Handyman to use the referral?

No — you can use the referral link to order Starlink directly from Starlink's website without booking an install. The free month is yours either way. That said, proper mounting and cable routing makes a big difference in signal reliability and weatherproofing, so many customers book both.

What does a professional Starlink installation include?

A typical install includes roof or pole mounting the dish at the optimal angle, routing the cable through the wall or conduit, connecting to your router, and a quick speed test to confirm it's working. I also advise on the best mounting location based on your roof and tree line.

How do I know if I have a clear sky view for Starlink?

Download the Starlink app and use the obstruction check feature — point your phone at different spots on your property. Ideally you want the northern sky clear of trees and structures. I can also check during a site visit before you order.
